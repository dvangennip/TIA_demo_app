Log for 2046, March, Friday

It's my tenth day out here in the desert, I miss the comfort of home and the absence of sand.

But I can't go back now, I'm still tracking the ship. It looks so serene as it floats over the landscape!

I caught a brief glimpse of its occupants this morning, green creatures with a scaley hairdo, wearing some kind of toga. I don't think they liked the desert either, after a look around they hurried back inside.

I hope to see more of them. I'll keep going tomorrow.

Log for 2046, March, Sunday

Once more they went out to inspect the landscape, this time to observe a tree or two. I could hear high pitched wheezing coming from one, the other brought out a stick-like device, rattled it and the wheezing stopped?

The afternoon was wasted looking for my glasses... I have to use a magnifier now to write this.

Before dinner, I made my own rattle sticks from stuff in Bunnings my bag. Next time, if I can get close, I'll give those a go. Maybe I'll learn how this works, what it does for them?

Log for 2046, April, Monday

I washed my clothes near the lake, unaware the spaceship had landed nearby.

We didn't see each other until they had gotten very close to the waterline and to me!

Oh! The aliens are as spooked as I am, and now they're rapidly hopping back to their ship, about to fly off! Shall I run after them and stop my notes?
